About Rangers and Rovers

Rovers and Rangers is the higher wing of Bharat Scout and guide. It is a voluntary and non-political educational movement for youths and open to all without distinction of origin, race or creed.
The college has a unit of twenty four+1 Rovers, twenty four+1 Rangers.
Kasturba Rangers and Mahatma Gandhi Rovers club MGM.
This unit is running since 2017 and registered.
